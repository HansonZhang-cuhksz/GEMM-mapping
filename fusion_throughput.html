What the winner does

The best config S3-N4-r2f folds all four vector ops into GEMM epilogues while keeping up_gate and down as separate, weight-amortized grouped GEMMs. It never re-reads weights, so its throughput scales with batch to 1.215 Mtok/s.

The rule: fuse the cheap vector ops into weight-amortized GEMMs; do not make the whole FFN intermediate-resident — that (F6) forfeits weight amortization and caps throughput ~4.9× lower.
